## 🧠 Pro OSINT Tips & Tricks

> ⚡ Combine these tools systematically for maximum info extraction:

1. **Start with identity clues** – emails, phone numbers, usernames (via Pipl, Spokeo, OSINT Framework).
2. **Pivot to image analysis** – scan profile pictures on TinEye or Yandex to locate connected footprints.
3. **Use domain + IP data** – run targets through Netcraft, WhoisXML, and VirusTotal to discover asset infrastructure.
4. **Geolocate with satellite tools** – verify addresses or physical boundaries via Google Earth or Sentinel Hub.
5. **Enumerate infrastructure** – pull active service banners with Shodan and Censys to find exposure vectors.
6. **Cross-reference everything** – build visual relationship charts using [Maltego](https://www.maltego.com) or automate workflow steps with [SpiderFoot](https://www.spiderfoot.net).
7. **Automate recon** – utilize command-line frameworks like [Recon-ng](https://github.com/lanmaster53/recon-ng) or [theHarvester](https://github.com/laramies/theHarvester) for scriptable gathering.
