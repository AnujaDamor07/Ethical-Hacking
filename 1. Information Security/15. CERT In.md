# 🇮🇳 CERT-In (Indian Computer Emergency Response Team)

## 📖 Introduction

**CERT-In** is the national cybersecurity agency of India under the **Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY)**. It is responsible for handling cybersecurity incidents, issuing advisories, and improving the overall security posture of India’s cyberspace.

# 🎯 Objectives of CERT-In

* Issue cybersecurity advisories and alerts
* Coordinate national-level incident response
* Provide technical guidance for cyber threat mitigation
* Strengthen India’s cybersecurity ecosystem
* Promote awareness and capacity building

---

# 🔑 Key Services

## 🚨 1. Cybersecurity Advisories & Alerts

* Real-time alerts on vulnerabilities and cyber threats
* Guidance on mitigation and patching
* Information on malware and exploit campaigns

---

## 🧾 2. Vulnerability Notes

* Technical details of reported vulnerabilities
* CVE-based analysis
* Impact assessment and remediation steps

---

## 🛡️ 3. Security Guidelines

* Best practices for organizations and government sectors
* Guidance for securing:

  * Networks
  * Applications
  * Cloud infrastructure
  * Critical systems

---

## 📩 4. Incident Reporting

* Platform to report cybersecurity incidents
* Enables CERT-In to assist in investigation and response
* Helps in national-level threat coordination

---

## 🎓 5. Training & Awareness

* Cybersecurity workshops and training programs
* Awareness initiatives for organizations and professionals
* Capacity building for secure digital practices

---

## 📊 6. Threat Intelligence

* Reports on emerging cyber threats
* Updates on Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs)
* Analysis of national and global cyber risk trends

---

# 🧠 How to Use CERT-In Effectively

* 🔍 Regularly check advisories for new threats
* 🛠️ Apply recommended security patches immediately
* 🚨 Report incidents using the official portal
* 📚 Follow published security guidelines
* 🎓 Participate in training programs for awareness

---

# 🎯 Key Takeaway

CERT-In plays a critical role in protecting India’s cyberspace by acting as a central authority for cybersecurity incident response, threat intelligence, and national security coordination.
